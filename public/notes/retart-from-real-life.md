*August 4th, 2025 — quiet thoughts from a dev's day*

---

Some days, you just pause everything.

No code. No plans. No expectations.  
Just the basics — clean your room, cook something simple,  
maybe rewatch an old anime or sit quietly with chai.  
That's real life too. Not everything needs to compile.

Lately, things have felt distant.  
People get busy.  
And maybe... I needed this space too.

---

I'll return to code — but not with pressure.  
Only when an idea hits like a bug I *want* to solve.  
Until then, I’ll fix what’s around me.

One task at a time.  
One thought at a time.

---

Just like coding —  
Sometimes, life needs a **soft reset**,  
not a full rebuild.
