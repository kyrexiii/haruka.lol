# another tuesday, another redesign

woke up around 10am today. 
sun was already too bright, but whatever.
brushed, took a shower. just the usual auto-pilot morning.

made coffee. 
drank it on an empty stomach before hitting some pushups.
needed the energy to wake my brain up. 
the house is pretty quiet these days.

opened the laptop and stared at the screen for a bit.
ended up redesigning the portfolio again. 
deleted a bunch of useless files, stripped away all the heavy text. 
just kept it raw and real. 
cleaning up a messy codebase kinda feels like cleaning up your mind. 
less noise is always better.

checked my phone a few times. 
mostly just random notifications. 
i know she’s buried in her books right now, studying hard. 
we don’t get to talk as much these days because of her schedule, but it’s fine. 

sometimes i just see her typing status and leave the chat open. 
reminds me of those older days when we'd just be on a call doing our own things. 
she'd be reading, i'd be coding. 
didn't even need to say words, just knowing someone is there on the other side is enough. 
that kind of comfortable silence is rare.

anyway. 
the new UI looks much cleaner now. 
the day wasn't anything crazy, just slow and steady.

sometimes simple days are the best ones.
